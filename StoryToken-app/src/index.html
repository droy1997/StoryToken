First edition novels are valuable and sought-after works of literature, but they are often subject to issues of authenticity, distribution, and preservation. Buyers and collectors may struggle to verify the authenticity of a first edition, and there may be limited access and distribution for these works. By creating an ERC-721 smart contract for a first edition novel, we can create a unique, digital representation of the book that can address these issues, providing a secure and verifiable record of ownership, greater accessibility and distribution, and a way to preserve the book for future generations.
